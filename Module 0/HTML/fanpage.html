Mariah Carey

Bio

Mariah Carey (born March 27, 1969) is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. Known for her five-octave vocal range, melismatic singing style, and signature use of the whistle register, she is referred to as the "Songbird Supreme" and the "Queen of Christmas". With sales of over 200 million records worldwide, she is also one of the best-selling music artists of all time.
